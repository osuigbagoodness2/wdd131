CleanTouch Laundry Website Plan

Overview

The CleanTouch Laundry website is a responsive and animated site designed for a professional laundry business. It highlights services offered, contact information, and business branding with a clean, modern design.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote CleanTouch Laundry’s services, attract local customers, and provide convenient access to business information like contact details, services offered, and location.

Audience

The website targets local residents, students, professionals, and businesses looking for reliable laundry services in Lagos, Nigeria.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #0077cc (blue)
  • Accent Color: #333333 (Dark Gray)
  • Text Color: #ffffff (White)

Typography

Font: Montserrat – Clean, modern sans-serif

Wireframe Pages

  • Home Page:
    • Hero image with headline
    • About Us section
    • Contact section
    • Footer with copyright
  • Services Page:
    • What We Offer
    • List of laundry services

Functionality & Features

  • Responsive design (mobile-friendly)
  • Scroll-based animations using ScrollReveal
  • Linked navigation
  • Images and branding
